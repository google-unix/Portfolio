## Canva Design Workflow

1. **Go to** [canva.com](https://canva.com) and search for "Instagram Post" or "LinkedIn Post" templates
2. **Filter by** "Social Media" → choose relevant template style (bold, professional, news-style, etc.)
3. **Apply brand colors:** Navy (#0A1628), Electric Blue (#00B4FF), White (#FFFFFF), Gold (#FFD700)
4. **Upload** Stackmin Services logo to Brand Kit in Canva for consistent use
5. **Use Canva AI** (Magic Write / Magic Design) to accelerate copy and layout generation
6. **Export** Instagram posts as 1080×1080 PNG; LinkedIn posts as 1200×628 PNG
7. **Schedule** via Meta Business Suite (Instagram) and LinkedIn's native scheduler
